Frequently Asked Questions

HOW MUCH STRAW CAN YOU PROVIDE?

We strive to give our customers whatever amount you need, although the answer may vary depending on the season, how many loads are requested ahead of yours, and of course the weather conditions.

HOW MUCH IS THE PRICE OF YOUR PINE STRAW?

The price depends on the type of straw you are purchasing and the location you are needing us to deliver it to. please contact us with your information and we will be happy to give you the best price for your needs.

DO YOU SELL ONLY TO CUSTOMERS OR IS IT AVAILABLE TO THE PUBLIC?

YES! We sell also to the public. Unlike other pine straw business, our work site is located in the Baxley city limits and is open to the public during standard business hours.

WHAT TYPE OF PINE STRAW DO YOU SELL?

The two common type of straw are slash which is our "standard" inventory and on the other hand we have long leaf "exclusive" straw. All of our straw is made by hand which is racked and cleared from sticks and pine cones. we also install both types of straw in any giving location. Give us a call for any question or comment you may have in mind, we'll be happy to have you an answer.

HOW LONG DO YOU HAVE TO WAIT FOR A LOAD WHEN REQUESTED?

As we work to have your load ASAP our timing may vary depending on the day your requested your load or how many loads have been requested ahead of yours. we can assure you that business is what we do best and we will not have you waiting long.

HOW FAR DO YOU DELIVER YOUR INVENTORY?

We deliver pine straw all over the southeastern united states. For any location that is not in range please contact us and we will go over further questions on how we will get our straw to you.

IS YOUR BUSINESS INSURED?

YES. The Pine Straw Man, LLC. is a fully insured and certified business. We have our fields as well as workers insured for any possible danger and keep a safer work job cite.

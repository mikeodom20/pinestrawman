THE PINE STRAW MAN LLC.

HERE WE WORK HARD EVERY DAY TO KEEP OUR WORKERS TO OUR CUSTOMERS HAPPY!!

The pine straw man llc. is a family owned an operated business that has been working hard for many years to meet our customers expectations in every way. We are a licensed business and meet all the requirements to keep our business as well as our employees insured and fully protected. Harvesting the best quality pine straw, we provide from deliveries to installations all around the southeast! Our main objective is to provide our customers with great quality straw and keep their customers satisfied as well.

  • We have heavy dude tractors hooked up to a bush hogs to clean our fields and extend our limits.
  • With our freight services, we haul 53' trailers which hold up to 1386 bales or our smaller 48' trailers which hold 1260 bales.
  • We work from hand racked and made bales in the pine straw fields and deliver the bales to your exact location as well as selling them here in our work site.
  • We work with farmers for timber leasing to obtain not only our land but a large quantity of acrs and keep the business running all year.
